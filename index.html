About Me

After studying Engineering for the past 15 years, I understood that numbers aren't just tools for measuring the world, they're a blueprint for understanding it. I'm fascinated by the idea that when grouped and transformed, data points can reveal patterns that explain completely different phenomena. From structural load-bearing capacity to population health trends, absolutly everything has a story. This fascination is what drives me to work and transform raw data, turning seemingly useless artifacts into powerful insights that open new windows so we can peek on the world.

As you'll see in my projects, I thrive on analytical challenges and love uncovering the narratives hidden in complex data. My approach is anchored in Statistics and rigorous methods, but I also heavily value the power of MAchine Learning to accelerate discovery. I believe we should always welcome a (mechanical) helping hand! Whether the data involves a large-scale classification problem or deep exploratory data analysis, I embrance the opportunity to learn emerging techniques and apply them to deliver innovative solutions.
